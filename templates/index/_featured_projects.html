HiveMind

HiveMind is a blockchain-based machine learning platform allowing participants to contribute data and train models in a fully decentralized way. It leverages federated learning to reduce overhead both in communication and computation. It combines a novel blockchain-based secure aggregation protocol with client-level differential privacy to prevent information leakage from the model parameters.
